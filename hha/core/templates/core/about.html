Welcome to American Baptist Church

We are a vibrant and diverse community of believers who come together to worship, learn, and grow in faith. Our mission is to spread the love of Christ and make a positive impact in our community and beyond. At Hope Hall Assembly, you'll find a welcoming atmosphere and a place where you can experience God's presence in a meaningful way.

Our Vision.

  • To make heaven.
  • To take as many people with us.
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

Our vision is to make heaven and ensure the inclusion of as many individuals as possible in this pursuit. This involves having a representative from the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) within every family across all nations, aiming for widespread spiritual presence and impact.

Our Mission.

  • To accomplish Vision 1, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • To accomplish Vision 2 and Vision 3, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.

Our mission is to make holiness our lifestyle to achieve Vision 1, while for Vision 2 and Vision 3, we plan to establish churches within a five-minute walking distance in developing countries and within a five-minute driving distance in developed countries' cities and towns. Our unwavering pursuit is to continue these efforts until the message of Lord Jesus Christ reaches every nation globally.

Our Core Values

Faith: Trusting in God's Plan

We believe that faith is the foundation of our journey. Through prayer, Scripture, and a deep reliance on God, we find strength, guidance, and purpose in our lives.

Community: Building Relationships

At HHA, we value relationships and genuine connections. We support and uplift one another as we grow together in faith, love, and fellowship.

Service: Making a Difference

Serving others is at the heart of our calling. We strive to make a positive impact in our community and world through acts of compassion, generosity, and outreach.

Excellence: Honoring God in All We Do

We are committed to honoring God by offering our best in everything we do. Whether in worship, ministry, or daily life, we aim to bring glory to His name.
